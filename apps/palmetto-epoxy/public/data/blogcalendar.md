## Overview

This extended 18-month editorial calendar balances epoxy flooring topics with deep coverage of: - Concrete polishing - Paver sealing - Driveway coatings - Resin countertops - Installation processes - Lowcountry climate impacts - Maintenance, repair, cost, ROI, and industry trends

Designed to demonstrate broad subject matter expertise and long-term SEO authority in the Bluffton, Hilton Head, and Beaufort markets.

* Add links to other blog posts or other content on palmetto-epoxy.com, particularly other blog posts on https://blog.palmetto-epoxy.com

* target content relevant to both residential and commercial customers
