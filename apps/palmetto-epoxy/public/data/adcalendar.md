# South Carolina Inspired Custom Color Blends

These 10 custom color names are designed for the Lowcountry market, utilizing regional landscape and historic colors:

* **Lowcountry Moss** : A deep green and grey flake blend inspired by Spanish Moss and local marshlands.
* **May River Rust**: A warm, earth-toned red and copper blend reflecting the iron-rich sediment of local waterways.
* **Charleston Slate**: A sophisticated dark grey-black with subtle green undertones, mimicking the iconic "Charleston Green" seen on historic shutters.
* **Haint Blue Shimmer**: A pale blue-green metallic finish inspired by the Gullah-Geechee tradition of painting porch ceilings to ward off spirits.
* **Rainbow Row Rose**: A playful, multi-colored pastel flake blend featuring pinks and corals found in Charleston’s historic district.
* **Palmetto Gold**: A vibrant tan and yellow-gold mix reflecting the state’s namesake tree and harvest season.
* **Sully’s Island Fog**: A soft, misty grey metallic floor with white swirls, evoking the early morning coastal atmosphere.
* **Marsh Grass Amber**: A multi-tonal tan, gold, and light green blend resembling the tidal grasses of the ACE Basin.
* **Battery Blue**: A deep, regal navy blue flake system inspired by the harbor views at The Battery.
* **Oyster Shell Pearl**: A crisp white and light grey flake blend with high-gloss topcoat to mimic the interior of local oyster shells.
